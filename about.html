As an acclaimed antiracism speaker and subject matter expert (SME) in racial justice, antiracism, and anti-bias education, Batanai Hawthorne has given keynotes and talks at conferences and universities. Batanai is known for his compassionate hands-on approach to topics from racial justice, anti-bias in education, and antiracism at home. He has collaborated with PBS, Drew Barrymore, Google Edu, The Mom Project, Rice University, and Association Montessori Internationale, among others. Batanai is talented at cultivating engaging in-person and virtual spaces.
