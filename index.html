Every Life Has a Story.
Identity through the One Life Foundation

Through the One Life Podcast and his work with the One Life Batanai Foundation, Batanai shares stories and hosts discussions focused on personal growth and cultural heritage. Batanai is known for his relatable and practical approach to exploring African identity, social connection, and the importance of living a purposeful life. He works alongside local entrepreneurs, travel enthusiasts, and community voices to encourage others to embrace their backgrounds and find meaning in their everyday journeys. Batanai is skilled at creating comfortable, open spaces for meaningful dialogue both online and in person.

Conversations that matter

  • Identity, belonging, and the stories we inherit
  • Race, power, and how history shapes everyday life
  • Raising conscious children in an unequal world
  • Culture, ownership, and the meaning of respect
  • Education, systems, and who they truly serve
  • Moving from diversity talk to real solidarity
  • Language, awareness, and the power of naming things
  • Faith, justice, and responsibility
  • And many more conversations
