Listen and Learn With Batanai
Book An Antiracism Speaker

As an acclaimed antiracism speaker and subject matter expert (SME) in racial justice, antiracism, and anti-bias education, Batanai has given keynotes and talks at conferences and universities. Batanai is known for his compassionate hands-on approach to topics from racial justice, anti-bias in education, and antiracism at home. He has collaborated with PBS, Drew Barrymore, Google Edu, The Mom Project, Rice University, and Association Montessori Internationale, among others. Batanai is talented at cultivating engaging in-person and virtual spaces.

Conversations that matter

  • Identity, belonging, and the stories we inherit
  • Race, power, and how history shapes everyday life
  • Raising conscious children in an unequal world
  • Culture, ownership, and the meaning of respect
  • Education, systems, and who they truly serve
  • Moving from diversity talk to real solidarity
  • Language, awareness, and the power of naming things
  • Faith, justice, and responsibility
  • And many more conversations
